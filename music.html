My Musical Exploration

Music has been something to connect me to my siblings. I have explored audio production through Ableton Live, using a Novation LaunchPad, and belt out a tune whenever I can. I picked up poetry to have something to sing, and I found a great way to process all that goes on in my mind.

Not only has music helped me to find deeper connection with my siblings, but it has opened my mind to see the world through a more creative lense, opening up emotions once closed off. In a sense, a kind of therapy

    I hope to convey some of my most valuable ideas, feelings, and thoughts to others one day through the medium of song.
