Current Pursuit: Fulfillment through movement

I am on a mission to explore all the ways we move.

Each of us moves in a unique way (How), for a unique reason (Why). We Each have our own set of experiences that influence both How and Why.

Through performance, we can only express through the limitations of the body. but these limits are capable of moving as well. As we train our body, we expand the limitations, capabilities, and in-turn the freedom to move in the way that brings us the fulfillment we seek.

"Reasons to Move"

I am on a mission to understand and experience as many movement styles as I can, adn to give attention to the nuance that the individual gives to the movements What does this nuance say about the artist? what does the movement do to fulfill the artist? For me:
I find fulfillment in the experience of a novel way of moving my body.
I find fulfillment in the connection of this shared experience; this shared movement.
I find fulfillment in the sharing of this movement with you... and the unlocking of movement within you to move throguht he world in new ways
Come experience the diverse individuality, the connection, and the beauty of many Artists and movers. identify the ways others move, and learn ways to move that you never thought possible.
